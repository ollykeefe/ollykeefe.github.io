Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 7, 2024

Hawthorne Grammar School we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website www.hawthornegrammar.edu.uk or interact with our school in any way.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information in various ways, including but not limited to: Personal Identification Information: Name, address, email address, phone number, date of birth, and other contact details. Educational Information: Student records, enrollment details, grades, attendance, and academic history. Payment Information: Details of payments made to the school, such as tuition fees, including credit card or bank account information. Usage Data: Information on how you use our website, including IP addresses, browser type, and operating system.

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes: To Provide Educational Services: Managing student enrollment, academic records, and communication regarding academic progress. To Improve Our Services: Analyzing data to improve our website, services, and communication. To Communicate: Sending newsletters, updates, and other school-related information. To Comply with Legal Obligations: Adhering to legal and regulatory requirements.

How We Share Your Information

We may share your information with: Service Providers: Third-party vendors who assist us with operational services, such as payment processing, data analysis, and communication services. Legal Authorities: When required by law or in response to legal requests. School Partners: With affiliated organizations for educational or administrative purposes, under strict confidentiality agreements.

Security of Your Information

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no internet or email transmission is ever fully secure or error-free. Please keep this in mind when sharing any information with us.

Your Rights

You have the right to: Access your personal information held by us. Request corrections to your personal information. Request deletion of your personal information under certain conditions. Opt-out of receiving communications from us.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies to enhance user experience. You can choose to accept or decline cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can modify your browser settings to decline cookies if you prefer.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website. Changes are effective immediately upon posting.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your personal information, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@hawthornegrammar.edu.uk Phone: +44 1234 567 890 Address: 1234 Elm Street, Springfield, SP 56789, United Kingdom
