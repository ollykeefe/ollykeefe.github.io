Hawthorne High Grammar School

[Read time: 5 mins]

Excellence in Knowledge, Character, and Community

Hawthorne Grammar School is a distinguished co-educational institution known for its rigorous academic standards and commitment to personal development. Nestled in the scenic town of Meadowville, the school has a long history of fostering intellectual curiosity, ethical leadership, and a sense of community among its students.

"Hawthorne' is a home for everyone" Mark Johnson, Deputy Head 2024

Hawthorne Grammar School prides itself on a curriculum that balances traditional academic disciplines with innovative teaching methods. The school offers a comprehensive range of subjects, including: Languages: English, Latin, French, Spanish, and Mandarin Sciences: Biology, Chemistry, Physics, and Computer Science Mathematics: From foundational to advanced levels, including Calculus and Statistics Humanities: History, Geography, Philosophy, and Religious Studies Arts: Music, Drama, Fine Arts, and Design Technology.

At Hawthorne High we strive for academic excellence and I am thrilled to welcome this years new cohort and fresh students into our community.

Regards, Fay Hawthorne

Headteacher at Hawthorne High Grammar School
